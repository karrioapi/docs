# Karrio Enterprise Self-Hosted

Built for large companies with significant data isolation requirements. Requires a contract.

[Book a call](https://karrio.io/contact-us) with our customer success team to get started with Karrio Enterprise.

## Deployment options

- Deploy Karrio using Kubernetes on your own cloud
  This requires knowledge of Kubernetes and your engineering team's time for deployment and ongoing maintenance.
  We are happy to provide reasonable support.

- Karrio Cloud Enterprise
  For most customers, the Enterprise version of Karrio Cloud will satisfy their compliance and privacy needs.
